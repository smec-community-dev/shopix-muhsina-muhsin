Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number when you create an account or make a purchase. We also automatically collect certain technical data like your IP address and device information through cookies to enhance your shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill your orders accurately.
  • To send security OTPs and order status notifications.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our platform.
  • To protect against fraudulent transactions and security threats.

3. Data Sharing with Sellers

As a multi-vendor marketplace, your shipping details are shared with the specific Verified Seller you purchased from to facilitate delivery. Sellers are prohibited from using this data for any marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Security & Payments

Shopix utilizes industry-standard encryption. We do not store your full credit card information; all financial transactions are handled by PCI-compliant third-party payment processors.
